About Swell Staked EtherSwell Ether (swETH) is an Ethereum liquid staking token (LST) by Swell protocol. It represents ETH staked on Ethereum, with the underlying ETH being verified by Chainlink Proof of Reserves. swETH is a repricing token that appreciates over time as staking rewards are earned. To restake swETH, simply deposit the token into the EigenLayer contract.
